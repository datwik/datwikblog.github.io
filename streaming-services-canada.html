In last few years, for the reasons unknown, Canadian video streaming industry exploded with numerous offerings, whether local or international.

Here we will explore few options available to you in directly over the internet. Some of them may be part of your TV subscription as well.

The list below is far from complete, there are more options available on the Streaming services in Canada DatWik topic. If you have any comments or would like to contribute - contact-us.

Free streaming

If you live in Canada, there is a growing segment of streaming services available to you absolutely free. Some of them require registration before accessing, You may be shown some ads along the way. In this table you can see the summary of all free services.

CBC Gem offers plenty of options to watch between all the shows, films and documentaries available on the service. Popular titles include Schitt's Creek, Sherlock, The Witch, This hour has 22 Minutes, The Great Canadian Baking Show and lot more (here is a longer list if you are interested).

National Film Board of Canada is featuring over 5,000 productions, Collection includes documentaries, animations, experimental films, fiction and interactive works. Also has a selection of animated films for both adults and children from talented filmmakers such as Cordell Barker, Chris Landreth, Ryan Larkin, Richard Condie, Ishu Patel, Caroline Leaf, and others. There are films here for all ages, in both French and English.

Knowledge Network is British Columbia's public educational broadcaster. We deliver what people deserve, enlightening and informative programming, from documentaries to drama, arts and culture to children's shows. Commissioned documentaries include: Paramedics: Life on the Line, This Mountain Life, Haida Gwaii: On the Edge of the World and Shut Up And Say Something.

Télé-Québec is a general-interest television channel that broadcasts French language programs, exclusive web series, and podcasts, such as Cuisinez! for the gourmet public, La Fabrique Culturelle, which highlights Quebec art and culture, Squat for 6-12 year-olds and Coucou for the very young.

TV5 Unis is a French-language channel that broadcasts general entertainment programming, with a particular focus on highlighting francophone communities outside Quebec.

Tubi is on demand, ad-supported video streaming service with larg library of content with 40,000 movies and TV shows, including a growing library of Tubi Originals, 100+ local and live news and sports channels, featuring content from every major Hollywood studio.

Plex has over 50,000 movies, shows and other content and over 250 free Live TV channels available to watch. It can search and discover content not only from it's own library, but from other (paid) streaming provider such as Netflix or HBO Max.

This is an aggregator website, collecting and ranking various documentaries from other websites like YouTube or Vimeo.

Kanopy's curated content includes movies, documentaries, foreign films, classic cinema, independent films and educational videos. You can access Kanopy using participating library or university card.

Hoopla provides movies, shows and other digital content such as ebooks, music and comics to library card holders.

The project contains over 4,000 restored polish film materials including feature classics by outstanding Polish filmmakers, films present in pop culture, documentaries and animated films.

Kids

Media content for kids comes in variety of options. There are plenty of kid oriented shows available from streaming providers with general content, such as: Netflix Kids, Tubi TV Kid Shows, Télé-Québec Jeunesse et famille, Disney+.

Websites listed below provide content oriented primarily to the younger audience.

Knowledge Kids is a children oriented section of the Knowledge Network service. You can watch episodes of Paw Patrol, S.M.A.S.H!, Treebies, Puffin Rock and many others for free.

Kanopy Kids offers a diverse selection of educational and entertaining content, including movies, TV series, animated storybooks, live action features, and animated favorites such as Sesame Street, Curious Geourge, Pigeon series and Science Max. In the Kanopy Kids section, you'll only see content that's suitable for children, so kids can browse freely. Note that you can only access Kanopy Kids using library card, it is not available through university.

Like on TV, kids can enjoy popular series online commercial-free including Paw Patrol, Gisèle’s Big Backyard, Annedroids, Dino Dan, Odd Squad, and Wild Kratts. tvokids is also the home for TVOKids’ educational games and apps, where kids can get interactive with their learning.

Kid oriented free content such as Om Nom, Astro Boy, Bigfoot.

Live TV

You can watch CBC Ottawa for free, rest of the regional channels and Olympic Channel are available upon paid subscription.

Live feed from Toronto City TV station.

Plex Live TV offers over 250 Live channels featuring live news (Reuters, Bloomberg TV, USA Today, Yahoo Finance, Euronews Francais), Sports (Racing America, Sports Grid, MMA TV, Warrior Wrestling, FTF Sports), Music (Stingray, Classic Rock, Qello Concerts), Movies (Kino Cult, Film Stream, Preview Channel) and many others.

Movies and shows

The following services provide wide variety of content: movies, tv shows, cartoons and documentaries. All of them are available for monthly and sometimes annual subscription. You can see their current (2022-09) price summary in this table.

Started as DVD mailing service, Netflix is one of the first streaming services in the market with large content selection for various audiences and original programming. You can watch here titles such as Young Sheldon, Stranger Things, Breaking Bad, Vikings and Downton Abbey.

Apple TV offers critically-acclaimed original content such as award-winning Ted Lasso and CODA, as well as many other shows including For All Mankind, Dickinson and Trying. An overview of content can be found on Rotten Tomatoes editorial.

Amazon Prime Video contains ever expanding content and it's own originals including The Grand Tour, Bosch and Fleabag. Guide to Amazon Prime Videos content on Rotten Tomatoes

Paramount Plus primary content is from of CBS Media Ventures, CBS Studios, Paramount Media (formerly Viacom Media Networks and ViacomCBS Domestic Media Networks), and Paramount Pictures, as well as original series and films.

Disney+ is home of Pixar, Marvel, Star Wars, original Disney classics as well as National Geographic and Star content.

Major content suppliers for Crave include Warner Bros. Discovery (HBO / HBO Max and Warner Bros. films) and Paramount (Showtime, Comedy Central, MTV, and the Star Trek franchise).

Тhe Criterion Channel offers classics and discoveries from around the world, thematically programmed with special features, on a streaming service brought to you by the Criterion Collection.

Mubi contains curated selection from new and award winning directors from around the world, spotlights on major film festivals, filmmaker retrospectives and own releases.

FlixFling carries indie darlings, classics, hard to find gems across every genre.

Acorn TV offers to watch new hit series and exclusive UK premieres of addictive dramas, thrilling mysteries, and warm-hearted comedies.

Britbox has binge mystery, comedy, drama, docs, lifestyle and more, from the biggest streaming collection of British TV ever.

Highball TV presents curated films from Cannes, TIFF, Sundance, and other festivals around the world.

Sundance now streams prestige drama, international thrillers, bone-chilling true crime, and other movies. New critically acclaimed originals, film favorites, and must-watch series added weekly.
