Software types

Why did the Word cross the road? To get to the other page.

Running a small business requires incredible investment of effort and time, apart from financial considerations. It's hard to find a single business here in York Region, as anywhere else, that does not rely on software to get through the day. Paperwork, taxes, accounting - it would be incredibly cumbersome to handle all those details by hand in the current fast pace environment.

If you ever prepared a document or made a spreadsheet, there is a high chance that you did it using the Microsoft Office package through Word or Excel. MS Office is incredibly powerful and sophisticated software, and the price for it reflects all that capabilities.

Now think back through the last few documents that you prepared using Word or Excel. How much menu items did you use. Granted there are very experienced users out there who love, treasure and use numerous features here. But the rest of us - it is buttons that we click dozen or so buttons on the ribbon to get the document ready to go.

If you have half dozen users in your office, you will be paying subscription fee comparable to another phone bill and it only going to increase as you grow. There is an alternative. If you are an industrious soul willing to invest some time to save on the MS Office subscription, take a look at Libre Office.

Overall it's capabilities are equivalent to the Office suite. Once you get used to the naming and layout of this software, you are getting a powerful tool that can take care of your daily paperwork needs. And the best part - it is absolutely free to install and use. Originally developed by Sun, before it was acquired by Oracle, this suite was released to open source community and is maintained as such ever since. Go ahead and try it out from the link below!
